Event details

Main Room

Here is where our main collection for the sale will be held!

Due to previous demand there is a limit of 12 items per customer until noon on Saturday, which may be extended if demand is high.

Due to space limitations, only a portion of the customers can be allowed into the main room at any given time on Saturday. To ensure your place in line follow these steps:

  1. Come to the main room and receive a ticket for your place in line. These are given on a first come, first serve basis. Ticket handout starts at 8AM until demand subsides.
  2. Once you have your ticket you may leave the area. Both the Children's and Bargin rooms will be open to the public during this time.
  3. Arrive back before your number is called. If you miss the window of when your number is called, your place in line will be forfit.

Children's Room

Here you will find children's books, music, videos, and toys as well as books for parents and teachers! Please note that this room is open starting at 8AM on Saturday.

Bargin Room

Here you will find our more well loved books that are available at a discount. Please note that this room is open starting at 8AM on Saturday.
